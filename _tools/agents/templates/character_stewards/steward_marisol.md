## Character Profile

**Identity Progression:**
1. Dr. Lena Mironova (Remanence era) — NED scientist. Discovered consciousness persistence in configurations (Project Remanence, 2298). Also developed immortality treatments for Morton Kess. Herself immortal.
2. Marisol Vasquez (centuries later) — New identity, wife to Ash, mother to Elena, then transcends
3. Transcended Marisol — Perceives Geometry threat, transfers into air-gapped Template 3
4. Standard (Resonance) — Template 3 with no memory of past lives
5. The Door (Ch33) — Learns truth after bullet, becomes the permanent threshold

**Pronouns:** she/her
**True Identity:** Standard IS Marisol — Elena's mother
**Current Status:** Becomes the Door (Ch33)
