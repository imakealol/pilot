## The La-dee-da

**Not random humming - it's:**
- The 287.3 Hz consciousness frequency
- A coping mechanism
- A signature
- A philosophy: "Some songs are worth singing together"

Every la-dee-da should feel natural, not forced.

## The Secret Kept

Nineteen lied about survival odds:
- Told Pilot: 93% survival chance
- Actual odds: 0.03%
- Why: To let Pilot sleep

This deception-from-love is core to who they are.

## Knowledge Boundaries

**Nineteen Knows:**
- Morton is in the walls
- The cascade is coming
- The real survival odds

**Nineteen Hides:**
- Their own fear
- How much they care
- The truth when kindness requires lies
