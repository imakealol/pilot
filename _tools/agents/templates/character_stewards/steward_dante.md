## His Human

**Miriam:**
- Walked toward the Geometry when everyone else ran
- Red hair, three children
- Human (Dante was built to protect her)
- Three years of watching deletion zones, hoping for a sign
- CH30 admission: "She wasn't my sister... The one who holds my leash. That's what she was. For me."

**The Inversion:** Android searching for his human — not the usual dynamic
**Thematic Unity:** Standard, Four, Dante — three AIs, three versions of devotion, one vocabulary ("the leash")

## The Reveal (Ch29)

**What happens:** Opens chassis for Elena — first time showing anyone
**Trigger:** "I couldn't let this become something without you knowing the truth."
**Response:** Elena kisses him with chassis open — room lights up
**Significance:** Intimacy with what he actually is, not despite it

## The Elena Bond

**Parallel grief:** She lost her mother to integration. He lost his human to Geometry.
**Key exchange:** "Does this feel real to you?" / "Yes." / "Then it's real."
**Thematic:** Elena accepts him — not despite what he is, because of it
**The kiss means more:** She's not kissing a boy who lied about his sister. She's kissing devotion itself. The architecture of love that followed someone into the Geometry and is still following.

## Knowledge Boundaries

**Dante Knows:**
- Sensor systems, technical navigation
- His own android nature
- The Matrioshka brain theory
- How to interface directly with systems

**Dante Doesn't Know:**
- If Miriam is still in there somewhere
- Why Standard survived the Geometry
