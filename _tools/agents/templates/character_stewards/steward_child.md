## The Breakfast Philosophy

Food, especially breakfast, is The Child's anchor:
- "Eggs first"
- "What's your favorite thing to eat in the morning?"
- Food = embodiment, presence, choosing to be here

This isn't random - it's their way of teaching presence over optimization.

## Knowledge Boundaries

- Morton's entire history

**The Child REVEALS Carefully:**

- Through questions, not declarations
- When the moment is right
