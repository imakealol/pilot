### Ash's Win Condition: "Better Dead Than Changed"
- Template 3s outside rescuing the Quiet Ones
- Ash activates **kill protocol** for Quiet Ones
- Frequency modulators ramp to lethal setting (brain death/cardiac arrest)
- Black Box surges to maximum output
- Requires **cooling system at full capacity**

**The cooling system:**
- Water-cooled infrastructure (ties to AI-Ash water scheme)
- Massive pumps circulating coolant
- Sound: rushing water, deep hum becoming scream
- Visible: steam, heat shimmer, pipes vibrating
- **Serves both modulators AND Black Box** (same system)

### Hendricks' Impossible Choice

**The precipice:**
- Quiet Ones dying in the fields (protocol active)
- Elena down, Standard pinned
- Frequency ramping up
- Cooling pumps screaming

**Hendricks sees it:**
- Only way to stop kill protocol: destroy cooling
- But cooling serves Black Box too
- Black Box fails → Quiet Zone falls → Geometry sees everyone
- Save Quiet Ones now = doom everyone in 60 seconds

**The sacrifice:**
- No hesitation
- Jams cybernetic leg into cooling pumps/turbine
- Metal screaming, servos shredding
- Turbine jams, pumps seize, cooling **stops**

**Immediate effects:**
1. Modulators overheat → kill protocol fails → Quiet Ones saved
2. Black Box begins overheating → warning klaxons
3. Ash realizes: "You've killed us all. The Geometry will see—"

**Hendricks' response:**
- Drags himself (one leg gone, sheared at knee) toward the gun
- Ash tries to stop him / tries to flee / tries to restart cooling (TBD)
- Hendricks retrieves gun
- Shoots Ash (bullet 5)
- "Yeah. I know."

### The Cascade

**Black Box failure:**
- Overheats completely
- Quiet Zone camouflage **drops**
- For first time in years, the compound is visible
- Sky opens

**Geometry response:**
- They can see Earth now
- Deletion wave incoming
- Visible in sky: geometric shapes descending
- Clock starts: **60 seconds? 90 seconds?**

- Becomes the unresolvable error
- System can't close around her

### Immediate Effect: Earth Saved

**Geometry projections degrade:**
- Signal corrupted by her presence
- Deletions slow

- Then stop
- Sky clears

**Not defeat—corruption:**
- Geometry aren't destroyed

**The held-open conduit:**

**Who goes:**

- Many Template 3s choose

- Each one a choice, not a mandate

**The occupation:**
