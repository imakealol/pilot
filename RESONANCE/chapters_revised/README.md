## The gate a chapter passes before it lands here
1. Fresh-spawned **steward** does a full rewrite from beats in the POV character's voice (briefed by title + path — CHAPTERS.yaml numbering is offset from filenames).
2. **Diff vs. the original** for plot/blocking/dialogue-meaning fidelity (no beats added/dropped/reordered).
3. **Continuity gate** (cold agent) — canon, AI-Ash-voice POV, the CONs.
4. **`voice_scan.py` + rubric read** — measurement (C1–C5, machine-vocab gate) where deterministic; voice/aside-density by read.
5. **Aside calibration** — spike-don't-sustain; thin idling asides, keep earned-beat spikes.
6. **Director sign-off.**
